Education

Wilfrid Laurier University

After deciding that I wanted to work towards something related to programming and computers, I wrote an application to several universities to study Computer Science. Wilfrid Laurier University (WLU) was the first university to respond, giving me an early offer and included a scholarship based on my highschool grades. Ultimately, I decided that WLU would be my choice and I started my first semester in Fall 2014. After 4 years of study and experiencing a home away from home, in Spring 2018 I stood with my fellow friends and graduates and accepted my B.Sc Honours Computer Science Degree.
