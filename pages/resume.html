What is Namsys?

Namsys is a fintech company that offers a variety of cash processing products on the cloud. I mostly worked on the Cash-in-Transit (CIT) product, a cloud based app for companies who deal with armoured car pickups/deliveries. Users create routes to their customers and the app tracks the progress of the routes quickly and efficiently. The web app works in conjunction with a mobile app allowing truck crewmen to scan codes, view stop details and request customer signatures, all of which are synced to the cloud.


Summary of Responsibilities

Features added to the web app were sometimes required on the Android app too, so I also improved the API communicating between the web and mobile side.
